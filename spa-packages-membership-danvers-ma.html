1) What are Spa Packages, Membership & Gift Cards?

Three Simple Ways to Make Self‑Care Happen

Think of these options as three different paths to the same goal: helping you (or someone you care about) get into the spa more consistently and get more value from each visit.

  • Spa Packages: curated multi-service experiences that turn a visit into a full reset—ideal for birthdays, couples, and longer “time off” appointments.
  • Membership: a structured monthly routine for guests who want consistent care, better long-term results, and an easier booking habit.
  • Gift Cards: flexible gifting that lets the recipient choose what feels best—massage, facials, Japanese Head Spa, or a package.

If you’re deciding between them: choose a package for a special day or a deeper reset, choose membership for consistency, and choose a gift card when you want to give someone freedom and a premium local experience in Danvers.

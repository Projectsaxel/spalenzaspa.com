Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Spalenza Spa, LLC. (“Spalenza Spa,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website, contact us, or book and receive services at our location in Danvers, Massachusetts. By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described here.

Who We Are

Spalenza Spa is a beauty salon and wellness spa located at 75 Newbury Street, Suite D, Danvers, MA 01923. You can reach us by phone at (978) 880-1400 or by email at spalenzaspaadm@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information gathered automatically when you use our website:

  • Contact and booking details: name, email address, phone number, appointment preferences, and any notes you share when you book, call, or message us.
  • Service information: the treatments you receive and preferences relevant to your care.
  • Payment information: processed by our booking and payment providers; we do not store full card numbers on our own systems.
  • Website usage data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, and referring pages, collected through cookies and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule, confirm, and manage your appointments.
  • To respond to your questions and provide customer support.
  • To process payments, memberships, and gift cards.
  • To send appointment reminders and, where permitted, offers and updates.
  • To improve our website, services, and overall guest experience.
  • To comply with legal, tax, and regulatory obligations.

Booking and Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to operate our business, including Phorest for online booking, memberships, and gift cards. When you book or pay through these services, your information is handled according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate (such as booking, payment, and communication platforms), or when required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of Spalenza Spa, our guests, or others.

Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as needed to provide our services, maintain business records, and meet legal obligations. When it is no longer required, we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize it.

Your Choices and Rights

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.
  • You may opt out of marketing messages at any time by using the unsubscribe link or contacting us.
  • You can manage cookies through your browser settings and our Cookie Policy.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at spalenzaspaadm@gmail.com.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Minors may receive services only with the consent and presence of a parent or legal guardian.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our website or services after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, contact us at spalenzaspaadm@gmail.com or (978) 880-1400.

1) What is Japanese Head Spa?

A Luxury Scalp Wellness Ritual

Japanese Head Spa is a premium scalp-focused treatment that combines cleansing, hydration, and targeted massage techniques to support scalp comfort, hair wellness, and deep relaxation. It’s not a standard shampoo appointment—and it’s not a typical massage.

Cleanse, Massage, Treat, Restore

Your session follows a calm, structured flow. The details may be customized, but the goal stays the same: bring your scalp back to balance and bring your body back to calm.

  • Warm water ritual: soothing rinses designed to relax the senses

Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Spalenza Spa, LLC. (“Spalenza Spa,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work, remember your preferences, and provide information to the site owners. Similar technologies include pixels, tags, and local storage.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: required for the website to function properly, such as page navigation and secure areas. The site cannot work correctly without them.
  • Performance and analytics cookies: help us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it. These may be set by analytics providers.
  • Functionality cookies: remember choices you make, such as preferences, to give you a more personalized experience.
  • Third-party cookies: set by external services we use, such as booking, maps, and social media platforms embedded on our pages.

Third-Party Services

Some cookies are placed by third parties that provide features on our website, including our booking provider (Phorest), mapping services, and social media platforms. These providers manage their own cookies according to their own policies.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Most browsers let you control cookies through their settings — you can block or delete cookies and choose to be notified when cookies are set. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect how our website functions.

  • Adjust cookie settings in your browser’s privacy or security menu.
  • Delete existing cookies stored on your device.
  • Use private or incognito browsing to limit stored cookies.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or the law. The “Last updated” date above shows the most recent revision.

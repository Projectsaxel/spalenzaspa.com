Skin Care in Danvers, MA

Facial & Skin Treatments in Danvers, MA — HydraFacial, Peels, Microchanneling & More at Spalenza Spa

Healthy skin rarely comes from one random facial once a year. At Spalenza Spa, we help guests in Danvers and across the North Shore build skin treatments around real goals: glow, hydration, anti-aging support, clearer texture, and a more confident everyday look.

Whether you want a HydraFacial before an event, a teen facial to support breakouts, dermaplaning for smoother makeup application, or a more advanced rejuvenation plan with chemical peels or microchanneling, our facial and skin treatment menu is designed to be practical, premium, and personalized.

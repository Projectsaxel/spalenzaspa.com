About Spalenza Spa — Licensed Beauty & Wellness Professionals in Danvers, MA

Spalenza Spa is a beauty salon & wellness spa in Danvers, MA, founded by licensed aesthetician and beauty professional Silvane Spalenza on August 25, 2017.

From day one, the vision has been simple: create a premium, detail‑oriented spa experience that still feels warm, personal, and locally grounded in the North Shore community.

Our Story

A Spa Built Around Real Guests

After years in the beauty and aesthetics industry, Silvane Spalenza wanted to open a spa in Danvers that felt both elevated and genuinely human. A place where guests from different backgrounds felt seen, heard, and cared for — whether they were coming in for a quick brow appointment or a full spa day.

Since opening in 2017, Spalenza Spa has grown through word‑of‑mouth, long‑term relationships, and repeat visits from clients across Danvers, Peabody, Beverly, Salem, and the wider North Shore MA area. Many guests consider the spa part of their monthly or seasonal routine, not just a once‑in‑a‑while treat.

The name “Spalenza” reflects this balance: a spa that combines beauty, wellness, and cultural influences in a way that still feels grounded and accessible for everyday life in Massachusetts.

Whether you arrive straight from Route 1 traffic or after a long shift, the goal is for you to leave feeling calmer, clearer, and more like yourself.

Service Philosophy

Results, Ritual, and Real Life

Spalenza Spa sits at the intersection of results‑driven services and calming rituals. We believe you should not have to choose between visible changes in your skin or body and a truly relaxing experience.

That is why you will find advanced facials, HydraFacial, laser hair removal, and microchanneling on the same menu as Japanese Head Spa, massage therapy, and curated spa packages.

  • Pair technology‑driven treatments with human, attentive touch.
  • Recommend realistic plans you can maintain — not just one‑time fixes.
  • Create an environment that feels calm, warm, and unhurried.
  • Help you look and feel like the best version of yourself, not someone else.

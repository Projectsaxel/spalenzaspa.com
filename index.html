About Spalenza Spa

A Multicultural Spa in Danvers

Spalenza Spa was founded by licensed aesthetician and beauty professional Silvane Spalenza with a clear intention: bring premium, detail‑oriented care to Danvers, MA in a way that feels genuinely warm and multicultural.

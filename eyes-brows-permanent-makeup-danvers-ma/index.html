Lash Lift, Brow Tinting & Permanent Makeup in Danvers, MA — Spalenza Spa

Small beauty details can make a big difference in how polished you feel every day. At Spalenza Spa in Danvers, MA, our lash, brow, and permanent makeup services are designed to simplify your routine while enhancing your natural features.

Whether you want a lash lift, lash lift plus tint, brow shaping, brow tinting, henna brows, combined lash and brow tinting, or longer-lasting brow definition through permanent makeup, we focus on balanced, wearable results that fit your face and lifestyle.

Service Overview

Lash Lift

Combined Lash & Brow Tinting

A practical combo for guests who want visible improvement in one appointment without overcomplicating their routine.
